Favorite BTS music videos


Fake Love released on May 18th 2018. With this song they wanted to relay the message of self love.
"We're saying that if you're not true to yourself, your love won't last forever. The love could be person to person,
it could be between me and myself" explained by leader Kim Namjoon(RM) himself.


Mic Drop released on November 24, 2017. With this song BTS expressed their hopes, dreams and confidence.
The action of dropping a mic after a speech or a statement soon became a symbol of ones confidence and thats what
they utilized in their song


Black Swan was released on March 4, 2020. This song touches upon the topic of when someone loses the passion for
creating. These lyrics express that once BTS lose the passion for creating music and the day that it feels like an obligation,
it would feel like death.


Film Out is one of their most recent single that released on April 1, 2021. Through this song BTS grapple with the bitterness of nostalgia
They tell the story of how hard it can be to let go of the past and look toward the future.
